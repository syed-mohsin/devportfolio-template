About Me

Hi! My name is Monica Torralba and I am a chemical engineering student from New Jersey Institute of Technology graduating in December 2017. I enjoy tackling creative and challenging projects like designing microfluidic devices and hydroponic gardens under my bed.

I am looking for a full-time engineering position, particularly something plant, aerospace, or energy related.

In the past few years, I’ve delved into mechanical and electrical projects in conjunction with my chemical engineering coursework and would love to continue working on interdisciplinary functions. I work hard to play hard, so in my free time, you can find me hiking in the mountains, painting figures, or cooking healthy colorful dishes. I hope that you find my projects useful and look forward to connecting with you!
